Tetris is a game where you have pieces that are different shapes fall down the game board and it is your goal to arrange the pieces by moving them around the board and rotating them to fill the rows of the game board. as you fill the rows they will be deleted and your score will increase. Once the pieces reach the top of the board then the game will be over.

This game is controlled using the arrow keys on your keyboard.

    Controls
  • Arrow Up --- Rotate the piece
  • Arrow Down --- Speed up drop of the piece
  • Arrow Left --- Move piece Left
  • Arrow Right --- Move piece Right

For each row that is filled your score will increase by 100 points!
